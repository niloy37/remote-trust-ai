# Demo Script

## 30-Second Setup

RemoteTrust AI helps global job seekers avoid fake, restricted, or low-quality remote jobs before they spend hours applying.

## Walkthrough

1. Open the landing page.
2. Click **Analyze a Job**.
3. Click **Try Verified Example**.
4. Run analysis and point out:
   - final trust score
   - Verified badge
   - high legitimacy, remote authenticity, eligibility, and quality scores
   - positive signals like salary, official hiring link, remote-first policy, and interview process
5. Click **Try Scam Example**.
6. Run analysis and point out:
   - Risky verdict
   - payment requests
   - Gmail or chat-app contact
   - no-interview urgency
7. Click **Try Restricted Remote Example**.
8. Show how a legitimate job can still be a poor match for a global applicant because it is US-only.
9. Seed or show the mixed demo batch.
10. Open **Curated Opportunities**.
11. Point out jobs scanned, curated jobs, review jobs, risky jobs removed, and average score.
12. Open one curated Job Trust Passport and show global fit, company evidence, and the reason it made the shortlist.
13. Switch to **Not recommended** and show how scams or hybrid traps were filtered out.
14. Open the dashboard.
15. Filter by verdict and applicant country.
16. Open a saved result and submit feedback.

## Closing

The MVP combines practical rule-based AI, NLP-style feature extraction, structured scoring, local persistence, a curated opportunity feed, and a feedback loop. It is demo-ready today and designed to grow into browser extensions, ATS verification, company reputation checks, and fine-tuned models.
